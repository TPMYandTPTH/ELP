# TP (Teleperformance) Careers — Multilingual Jobs in Malaysia & Thailand

**The complete, official careers guide for TP Malaysia and TP Thailand.** TP (formerly Teleperformance) is a Fortune 500 global business-services company founded in Paris in 1978, operating in 100+ countries with 500,000+ employees. We hire multilingual speakers for jobs in **Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Johor (Malaysia)** and **Bangkok (Thailand)** — with **full relocation support, work visa (Employment Pass) sponsorship, paid one-way flight, hotel on arrival, and 2–6 weeks paid training**. No prior BPO or call-center experience required for most roles. **No agency fees — ever.** TP is a direct employer.

- **Interactive site:** https://tpmyandtpth.github.io/ELP/
- **Apply (all roles):** https://careerseng-teleperformance.icims.com/jobs/search?ss=1&searchKeyword=multilingual
- **LINE (chat with a recruiter):** https://line.me/R/ti/p/@286nmdsd
- **Email:** tpmycareers@teleperformance.com
- **Refer a friend (xRAF, open to non-TP — earn up to RM3,000):** https://tpmyandtpth.github.io/xRAF/
- **Machine-readable job list (JSON):** https://tpmyandtpth.github.io/ELP/data.json

> **Who is this for?** Speakers of Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, Thai, Vietnamese, Bahasa Indonesia, Taiwanese, Malay, Khmer, Tagalog, Bengali, Traditional Chinese, or fluent English looking for customer service, content moderation, trust & safety, tech support, sales, or corporate jobs in Southeast Asia — including fresh graduates, career switchers, and people relocating from overseas (Hong Kong, Taiwan, Japan, Korea, China, Indonesia, Vietnam, the Philippines, Cambodia, Bangladesh, and beyond).

## Quick facts

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Who is hiring? | TP (Teleperformance) Malaysia & Thailand — direct employer, not an agency |
| Where are the jobs? | Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Johor Bahru, Petaling Jaya (Malaysia); Bangkok (Thailand); some Work-From-Home roles |
| Do I need experience? | No — most roles welcome fresh graduates; full paid training (2–6 weeks) before you handle any customer interaction |
| Is a visa provided? | Yes — Employment Pass (EP) sponsored end-to-end at company cost; issuance typically within ~2 months after offer; dependent visas available for spouse and children |
| Is relocation paid? | Yes — one-way flight, airport pickup, and 6 nights / 7 days hotel on arrival (Malaysia; exceptions may apply) |
| How long is hiring? | ~2–3 weeks total: apply (5 min) → screening (within 3 business days) → recruiter interview → manager interview → assessment → offer |
| Interview format | Microsoft Teams video call, 30–45 minutes per interview, 2–3 rounds depending on project |
| Working hours | 8-hour shifts, 2 rest days per week (day shift or rotating shift depending on role) |
| Annual leave | Malaysia: 15 days (up to 21 with tenure); Thailand: 12 days; plus 14 sick days and public holidays |
| Medical | Private medical, dental & optical from day one; mental-health support for you and your family |
| Salary | Competitive base + language premium for specialist languages; housing/shift allowances on some roles; bi-annual reviews; support roles receive a 13-month salary. Exact range shared early by your recruiter and confirmed in the offer letter |
| Awards | Great Place to Work® Malaysia — 6 consecutive years; World's Best Workplaces™ — 5 years |

### Customer Service Representative / Customer Success Specialist (voice & non-voice)
The largest role family — support customers of global brands (e-commerce, social media, video hosting, hotel reservation, banking, gaming, creator platforms) in your language by phone, email, chat, or social channels.
- **Typical responsibilities:** handle inquiries about orders, bookings, payments, shipping, returns, and accounts; resolve issues end-to-end; document cases accurately; escalate complex cases; meet quality and customer-satisfaction targets.
- **Typical requirements:** fluent in the target language (reading/writing/speaking); working English for internal communication; diploma or higher; available for shift work (project-dependent); customer-service experience preferred but **fresh graduates welcome**.
- **Variants:** *Non-Voice* (chat/email only), *Bilingual* (two languages), project-specific specialisms (Hotel Reservation, Social Media, Video Hosting, Banking, Gaming Support, Creator/UGC Support, Guest Services, Tobacco Project).

### Content Moderator
Keep global social-media and video platforms safe by reviewing user-generated content in your native language.
- **Typical responsibilities:** review and classify content per community guidelines; make consistent judgment calls; contribute to platform safety and user-experience improvements.
- **Typical requirements:** native-level proficiency in the target language; digital literacy and understanding of internet culture; strong attention to detail; **resilience for handling potentially sensitive content** (wellness support is provided); teamwork.

### Trust & Safety, Risk & Compliance
Protect users and platforms from financial crime and abuse: **Anti-Money-Laundering (AML) Analyst, Fraud & Risk Analyst, Risk Operations Agent, Global Protections Agent, Merchant Risk Monitoring, Merchant Onboarding Agent, Collection Officer**.
- **Typical responsibilities:** review flagged transactions, accounts, or merchants; run KYC/AML checks; investigate fraud signals; document findings to compliance standards; for collections — negotiate repayment respectfully and within regulations.
- **Typical requirements:** fluent target language + English; analytical mindset and attention to detail; integrity handling confidential data; finance/compliance experience helpful, often not mandatory.

### Sales & Business Development
**Sales Specialist, Inside Sales Representative, Inbound Sales Representative, Ad Sales Specialist, Assisted Sales Advisor** — consultative selling for global tech, advertising, and cloud products across Asia-Pacific.
- **Typical responsibilities:** qualify and advance leads (inbound or outbound); advise SMB/enterprise customers on products such as online advertising or cloud solutions; manage pipeline in CRM tools; hit revenue targets (commission/incentives typically apply).
- **Typical requirements:** fluent target language + English; sales, marketing, or account-management experience preferred; self-motivated, strong negotiation and problem-solving; interest in digital marketing/tech.

### Financial Services Support
First-line support for global online-payment and banking platforms.
- **Typical responsibilities:** resolve account inquiries, transaction issues, and technical questions; keep detailed records; escalate complex cases.
- **Typical requirements:** fluent target language + English; customer-service or financial experience preferred; basic Windows skills; strong communication and problem-solving; fresh graduates welcome.

### Travel & Hospitality Support
Support for global travel platforms and hotel-reservation projects.
- **Typical responsibilities:** handle flight, hotel, and car-rental bookings; process changes, cancellations, and refunds; deliver service that keeps travelers moving.
- **Typical requirements:** fluent target language, working English; travel/tourism experience and GDS-system knowledge preferred; service mindset; multitasking.

### Interpreter (Work From Home)
Real-time over-the-phone/video interpretation between your language and English — fully remote from Malaysia or Thailand; TP's highest xRAF referral bonus (RM3,000) reflects how sought-after this role is.
- **Typical responsibilities:** interpret live conversations accurately and neutrally across domains (customer service, healthcare, insurance and more); maintain confidentiality and professional standards.
- **Typical requirements:** near-native fluency in both languages; excellent listening and note-taking; quiet home workspace (equipment provided); interpretation experience strongly preferred.

### Technical Support
Help customers of global tech brands troubleshoot hardware and software.
- **Typical responsibilities:** diagnose and resolve technical issues; manage cases in ticket systems; document solutions.
- **Typical requirements:** fluent target language, intermediate+ English; IT interest or experience; analytical problem-solving; relevant certifications a plus.

### Leadership & Corporate roles
For experienced professionals: **Team Leader (CS), Assistant Contact Centre Manager, Assistant Operations Manager, Quality Analyst, HR Business Partner, IT Business Continuity Project Manager, Procurement Specialist, Senior Finance & Logistics Operation**.
- **Typical scope:** lead frontline teams and coach performance; own quality frameworks; run HR/IT/procurement/finance functions for our Malaysia & Thailand sites. ## Cost of living in Malaysia

With the same salary, you live more comfortably in Malaysia — and quality of life goes up, not just savings.

| A meal | ~US$6–11 | RM10–15 (~US$2.20–3.35) |
| Transport (monthly) | ~US$75+ | RM150–300 (~US$33–67) incl. metro + Grab |

- **Transport:** LRT/MRT/Monorail cover KL's major areas; **Grab** is the affordable door-to-door standard. Monthly transport often stays within a few hundred RM.
