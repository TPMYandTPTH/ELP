FAQ

Is it okay if I'm not confident in English?

Of course! There are many positions where your native language is primary. After joining, you can improve step by step through Language Bootcamp and GoFluent. Interviews are also mainly conducted in your language.

I'm worried‚Äîit's my first time going abroad.

Don't worry! Initial support is complete including work visa support, airport pickup, arrival hotel (Malaysia), etc. We'll also guide you through finding housing in your language.

Can I go with my family?

After obtaining work visa, dependent visas for spouse and children can be applied for (varies by country and employment conditions, we'll guide you individually).

How long from application to joining?

The entire process is about 2-3 weeks (documents‚Üí2 interviews‚Üífinal result). Work visa takes about 1-2 months after offer.
