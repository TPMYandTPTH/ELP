Kuala Lumpur (KL)

A multicultural city where urban energy meets natural scenery. Convenient transport, diverse dining, abundant entertainment.

Transportation

Metro, buses, and Grab app available. Central location for quick and easy commutes.

Dining

From street food to local cuisine, Japanese, and cafes. Affordable dining with new discoveries daily.

Fitness & Recreation

Apartments with gym and pool, nearby parks and sports facilities. Recharge body and mind after work.

Penang

An island close to the sea with a relaxed pace of life. Quiet environment, affordable living, beautiful nature.

Transportation

Grab app is the main transport. Easy access to beach or city center.

Food Culture

Thriving street food, food courts, and cafe culture. Diverse food choices, different flavors daily.

Leisure Life

Beach walks, water activities, cafe meetups. Relaxed lifestyle is the norm.

Bangkok

Southeast Asia's most international city. Advanced transport, excellent services, abundant choices.

Transportation

Well-developed metro system connecting major areas. Grab app is also affordable and convenient.

International Community

International restaurants, supermarkets, and multilingual healthcare. Easy adaptation for newcomers.

Leisure Life

Large shopping malls, unique restaurants, diverse events. Plenty to enjoy after work.
