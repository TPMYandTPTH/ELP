Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers before applying

Can I apply even if my English isn't strong?

Yes, many positions are native language-focused. After joining, we offer language learning support programs like GoFluent to help you gradually improve your English skills.

Is the visa process difficult?

The company handles the visa application, so don't worry. We guide you through document preparation in your language and support the entire process from application to approval.

How do I find accommodation?

We introduce you to local real estate agents and provide advice during onboarding. For Malaysia, we provide temporary accommodation so you can take your time finding a place.

Are there shift work positions?

It depends on the project. Some positions are day shifts only, while others have rotating shifts. We'll explain the schedule during the interview process.

Can I work using only my native language?

Many positions are native language-focused. Basic English may be needed for internal communication in some cases, but language support is available.

What is a casual chat?

It's not an interview but an informal online conversation about working and living at TP, taking about 30 minutes. Those considering a career change are also welcome.
