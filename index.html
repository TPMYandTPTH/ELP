About TP

Global leader in BPO, offering career opportunities for multilingual talent

TP (Teleperformance) is a global leader in Business Process Outsourcing (BPO), founded in Paris, France in 1978. We currently operate in over 100 countries worldwide with more than 500,000 employees.

We combine cutting-edge technology with exceptional talent to deliver world-class customer experience services to global brands. We are actively recruiting multilingual talent in Malaysia and Thailand.

Whether you're a fresh graduate or experienced professional, we offer a diverse and inclusive work environment with comprehensive career development opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply even if my English isn't strong? +

Yes, many positions are native language-focused. After joining, language support like GoFluent is provided.

Is the visa process difficult? +

The company handles the visa application. We guide you through document preparation in your language.

How do I find accommodation? +

We introduce you to local real estate agents and provide temporary accommodation (Malaysia) so you can take your time.

Are there shift work positions? +

It depends on the project. Some positions are day shifts only, others have rotating shifts. Details explained during interview.

What is a casual chat? +

It's not an interview but an informal 30-minute conversation about working and living at TP. Career explorers are welcome.

Can I go with my family? +

After obtaining a work visa, dependent visas for spouse and children can be applied for.
