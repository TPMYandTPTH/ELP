questions§ tap to expand

Questions.

Do I need BPO experience?add

No. We value language skills and communication. Full paid training — 2 to 6 weeks — before you handle any customer interactions.

Who handles my visa and relocation?add

We do — end to end. Employment Pass, one-way flight, airport pickup, and initial accommodation.

What languages are you hiring for?add

Mandarin, Japanese, Korean, Thai, Cantonese, and English — with new lines opening regularly.

What's the salary range?add

Competitive, with your language premium. Your recruiter shares the range early. Final terms are confirmed in your offer letter.

What does a typical workday look like?add

8-hour shifts, 2 rest days per week. Pantries, prayer rooms, break areas. After work — hawker food, malls, or beach.

Is Malaysia safe and affordable?add

One of Asia's most expat-friendly countries. Street food from RM5 (~US$1), rent RM800–1,500. 100+ nationalities on our team.

Can I grow into leadership?add

Many team leads started frontline. Structured promotions, mentorship, leadership academies, GoFluent training.
